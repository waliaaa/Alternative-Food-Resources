Proposed Materials And Methods

Insect Farming

Proposed Materials for insect farming includes specialized containers or enclosures designed for controlled environments. These containers are typically made from materials like food-grade plastic or metal to ensure hygiene and durability.

Proposed Methods focuses on creating controlled environment that optimise insect growth. Insects like mealworm or black soldier flies are chosen for their high protein content in converting organic matter to biomass. This sustainable approach to farming has the potential to adress food security challenges.

Vertical Farming

Proposed Materials for vertical farming include sturdy vertical structures like metal rocks or shelves to support plant-growth, specialized grow lights provides light spectrum for photosynthesis, hydroponic or aeroplanic system are used.

Proposed Methods cultivates crops in stacked layer indoors. It utilizes controlled environment with specialized lighting, hydroponic systems and precise climate control. This approch conserves resources.

Proposed Materials for Aquaponics consists of fish tanks, plants, water pumps, and tubing.

Proposed Methods is to combine aquaculture (Fish Farming) and hydroponics(Soilless Plant Cultivation) in symbiotic system fish waste fertilizes the plants, and plants purify the water.
